Executive Summary

Welcome! This website hosts four sample products for the Human Rights Watch Environmental Justice division to display, interact with, and create spatial data. All the products address deforestation and air pollution in the Amazon Rainforest.


Product 1 is designed to help decision-making and promote user interaction with data.

Product 2 supports coordination during a project by displaying on-going conservation efforts.

Product 3 tells a story about environmental justice work conducted by the Human Rights Watch.

Product 4 is a web form for crowd-sourcing data.
